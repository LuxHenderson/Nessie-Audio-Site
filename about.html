- About

Nævermore is a modern Hard Rock/Metal band born in the heart of Central Illinois. Forged from diverse backgrounds but driven by the same relentless passion, we create music built for the stage—heavy, melodic, and unapologetically alive. What started as a shared love for live performance has evolved into a powerful collaboration defined by raw energy, emotional intensity, and musicianship that refuses to sit still. Whether it's the studio or the stage, Nævermore is here to push harder, play louder, and connect deeper...
                                                       ...This is just the beginning.
